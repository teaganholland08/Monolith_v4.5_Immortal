## üìã EMERGENCY PROCEDURES

### Abscess Drainage

```markdown
## ABSCESS DRAINAGE PROTOCOL

**Indications:**
- Visible swelling in gum
- Severe pain
- Fever
- Difficulty swallowing

**Procedure:**
1. Sterilize scalpel blade (#11 or #15)
2. Apply topical anesthetic (clove oil)
3. Make small incision at point of maximum swelling
4. Allow pus to drain
5. Irrigate with saline solution
6. Pack with iodoform gauze
7. Start antibiotics immediately (Amoxicillin 500mg 3x/day)

**Post-Care:**
- Warm salt water rinses (every 2 hours)
- Continue antibiotics for 7-10 days
- Monitor for spreading infection
```

### Tooth Extraction

```markdown
## TOOTH EXTRACTION PROTOCOL

**When Necessary:**
- Severe decay beyond repair
- Abscess not responding to antibiotics
- Fractured tooth below gum line

**Procedure:**
1. Inject lidocaine around tooth (4 injection sites)
2. Wait 10 minutes for numbness
3. Use elevator to loosen tooth from socket
4. Rock tooth back and forth with forceps
5. Apply steady pressure and extract
6. Pack socket with gauze
7. Bite down for 30 minutes

**Post-Care:**
- No smoking/drinking through straw (dry socket risk)
- Soft foods for 3 days
- Salt water rinses after 24 hours
- Ibuprofen for pain
```

## üß™ DIY PHARMACEUTICAL PRODUCTION

### Willow Bark Aspirin

```markdown
## WILLOW BARK TINCTURE (Natural Aspirin)

**Ingredients:**
- White willow bark (dried)
- 80-proof vodka or ethanol

**Process:**
1. Fill jar 1/3 with dried willow bark
2. Cover with alcohol
3. Seal and store in dark place for 4-6 weeks
4. Shake daily
5. Strain through cheesecloth

**Dosage:**
- 1-2 teaspoons for pain/fever
- Contains salicin (converts to salicylic acid)
```

### Pine Resin Salve (Antibiotic)

```markdown
## PINE RESIN ANTIBIOTIC SALVE

**Ingredients:**
- Pine resin (collected from trees)
- Coconut oil or beeswax
- Olive oil

**Process:**
1. Melt coconut oil/beeswax (1:1 ratio)
2. Add pine resin (1 part resin to 3 parts oil)
3. Heat gently to combine
4. Strain through cheesecloth
5. Pour into containers

**Uses:**
- Cuts, scrapes, burns
- Natural antibiotic properties
- Promotes healing
```
